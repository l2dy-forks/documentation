## AI inference endpoints now available

You can now hit our OpenAI compatible API endpoints through AI Inference on Ubicloud console. 

**There are 7 models available, all open source;**

* Ds-r1-qwen-32b (Text-generation) 

* llama-3-1-8b-it

* E5-mistral-7b-it (Embedding)

* Llama-3-2-3b-it

* Llama-3-3-70b-it

* qwen-2-5-14b-it

* qwq-32b-preview

**Models are priced between \$0.10 - \$0.60 per million tokens:**
